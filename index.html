Purpose:

Technologies:

Description:

Maíz brings light to the 15 mile stretch of Nolensville Road in South Nashville. It highlights al 41 Latino food establishments to the greater population and encourages user feedback to get consumers excited and more comfortable with the variety of restaurants available. Each food establishment shows its individual details, messaging system for individualized user comments, and a voting system for 6 various food/drink categories. Use By.

Purpose:

Nash Hack Weekathon.

Technologies:

React. JSX. CSS. Visual Studio Code. Github

Description:

Use By was created at a 48 hour hackathon in October 2018 in Nashville. Our team won the People's Banana award for most voted app. Its primary purpose is to help food consumers be conscious of their personal food waste. Once a user picks or adds a food item to put in the refrigerator, an automatic use by date for that specific item counts down. A user can use the item or waste the item. Once an item is wasted, it is tallied with similar items and displayed by most wasted descending. An overall money lost on unused food is highlighted to show the user their loss.

Recap.

Purpose:

Back-end Capstone

Technologies:

.Net. C#. JavaScript. Chart.JS. Entity. Identity.MVC. CSS. Visual Studio. Github

Description:

Recap is a survey application that provides two different interact experiences for a survey owner and survey user. An owner can log in and create, edit, revise, delete, and publish surveys. Once published, only owners can delete and review results about the surveys, ensuring that information is stored securely. After publication, the user can answer the survey questions anonymously. Once the anonymous user submits their recap, the data is translated into visual charts for the owner as feedback.
